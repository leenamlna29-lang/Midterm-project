ABOUT RHODE

Our mission and values

OUR MISSION

Our Pocket Blush comes in a variety of stunning shades, carefully curated to complement every complexion. Each shade is formulated with premium ingredients to deliver a natural flush that lasts all day. We believe beauty should be accessible, and quality shouldn't be compromised.

FOUNDED BY HAILEY BIEBER

RHODE Pocket Blush was founded by Hailey Bieber with one goal: to create the perfect blush that fits your lifestyle. With a focus on portability, pigmentation, and inclusivity, RHODE delivers beautiful color for all.

Hailey's personal mission was to design a blush that's not just beautiful, but practical too. The Pocket Blush is perfect for on-the-go touch-ups and comes in shades for every skin tone.
